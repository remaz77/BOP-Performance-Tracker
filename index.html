How Scores Are Calculated

📊 Quantity Score (30% of Overall)

Based on confirmed work items only. Each item gets a weight:

  • On Time → weight = 1.0
  • Completed Early → weight = 1.2 (bonus)
  • Completed With Revised Timeline / Completed with Delay → weight = 1.0 if reason is "Outside Our Control", otherwise weight = 0.8

Total weight is compared to a target of 3 (three normal items). Score = min(100, (totalWeight / 3) × 100).

✅ Quality Score (40% of Overall)

Average of manager assessments across four metrics: Accuracy, Efficiency, Timeliness, Proactivity (each rated 1–10). Score = (average rating / 10) × 100.

💡 Ideas & Innovation Score (15% of Overall)

Percentage of ideas that are Approved or Implemented. Score = (approved ideas / total ideas) × 100. (0 if no ideas.)

⭐ Feedback & Collaboration Score (15% of Overall)

Based on feedback received. Rating (1–5) converted to points (rating × 20). Gold badge adds 3 points, Silver adds 1. Score = min(100, average rating points + badge bonus).

🏆 Overall Score (0–5 scale)

Weighted combination: (Quantity×0.30 + Quality×0.40 + Ideas×0.15 + Feedback×0.15) / 20. Star rating derived from overall score.
